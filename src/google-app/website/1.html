      By S K Goyal
      Amazon Verified Purchase
      Yesterday only I got my passport from Amazon at best possible price on internet (Rs. 4320/-). Other online stores were offering the same for Rs. 4800+ only, whereas offline, my local vendor was asking for 5.5k+ for the same. So, it was really a good bargain at amazon.

      I read most review before ordering. I really wanted to know if the USB cable supplied support USB 3.0 (may be it was a stupid question in my mind), but no one answered it before. Anyways, I ordered it and found that the cable indeed support both USB 2 and USB 3.

      Anyways, here is my first ever review at amazon.

      Pros:

      1. Very light weight - you can practically keep it in your pocket.
      2. USB cord compatible with USB 2.0 as well as USB 3.0
      3. Very good data write speed - I transferred (using USB 3.0) about 286GB data (over 21000 video files) in about 1 hour and 20 minutes, which roughly gives a speed of 60mb per second. It should be more fast if the number of files were less. :)
      4. Nice Apps for drive security, backup and testing - although I have not used them thoroughly.
      5. No vibration or heating up of the drive after constant use for more than 2 hours.

      Cons:

      1. Many reviewers have complaint of small USB cable - for me it was really fine.
      2. I strongly felt the need of some pouch / carry bag. It could be a very nice addition to the product.
